FREELANCING AND SELF-EMPLOYMENT BASE RATES
Average time to land first paying freelance client: 3-6 months
62% of new freelancers report income instability in their first year
Only 35% of people who say they will go freelance are still doing it after 2 years
Top reason freelancers return to employment: inconsistent income (58%)
Average new freelancer works 6 months before matching their previous salary
Most freelancers underestimate their effective hourly rate by 40% when factoring unpaid admin time
Healthcare, taxes, and equipment costs add 20-30% to the cost of self-employment vs employment

STARTUP AND BUSINESS FAILURE RATES
90% of startups fail within 10 years
38% fail due to running out of cash before finding product-market fit
35% fail because there was no real market need for what they built
20% fail due to team and co-founder problems
Median time from founding to startup failure: 20 months
Side projects: 95% are abandoned within 3 months of starting

CAREER CHANGE REALITIES
Average job search duration after quitting without another offer: 3-6 months
People who quit without a job lined up take 40% longer to find new work
Only 1 in 4 people who plan to figure out what they want during a sabbatical report genuine clarity after 2 months
People systematically underestimate monthly living expenses by an average of 30%
Gap periods longer than 6 months require explicit explanation to most hiring managers

RELOCATION AND MOVING
40% of people who move cities for lifestyle reasons return within 3 years
60% report unexpected social isolation in the first 6 months after moving somewhere new
70% of people moving cities underestimate the true cost of living when accounting for setup costs
Finding a comparable social network after moving typically takes 1-2 years

RELATIONSHIP AND SOCIAL DECISIONS
Couples who separate and reconcile have a 75% chance of separating again within 5 years
27% of people who cut off family members regret the decision within 2 years
6 months is the minimum recommended waiting period before major life decisions after a breakup
People overestimate how much relationship changes will improve their overall happiness by an average of 2x
