HOW HIRING AND JOBS ACTUALLY WORK
LinkedIn cold application response rate: 2-3% on average
Most job offers come from referrals and warm networking, not cold applications
Salary negotiation: 70% of employers expect negotiation but only 37% of candidates attempt it
Internal transfers are 3x more successful than external applications for equivalent roles
Job titles and responsibilities vary enormously across companies and mean little on their own
Background checks now include social media screening at 70% of companies

HOW MONEY AND PERSONAL FINANCE WORK
Emergency fund minimum for self-employed or career-changers: 9-12 months of expenses
Effective rental income yield after maintenance, vacancy, and management: 4-6% not gross rent
Stock market average annual return over 30 years: 7-10% inflation-adjusted, not 20-30%
Most people overestimate investment returns by 2-3x in optimistic planning scenarios
Lifestyle inflation: people who get raises spend 70-80% of the increase within 6 months
Credit card minimum payments result in paying 3x the original purchase price over time

HOW LEARNING AND SKILL-BUILDING ACTUALLY WORK
Time to job-ready programming proficiency starting from zero: 6-12 months of dedicated daily practice
Online course completion rate: only 3-6% of enrolled students actually finish MOOCs
Language fluency hours required: 600-750 for similar languages, 2200+ for distant languages
Expertise in any complex domain requires approximately 10,000 hours of deliberate practice
Learning a skill recreationally versus professionally requires fundamentally different approaches

HOW ONLINE AND CREATIVE BUSINESSES WORK
90% of new online stores fail within the first 120 days
Average time to first dollar of revenue for a new content creator: 12-18 months
Monetization on YouTube requires 1000 subscribers and 4000 watch hours before any revenue
Dropshipping profit margins after advertising costs: 10-20% at best
Most creative freelancers earn below minimum wage in their first year when accounting for all hours worked

HOW HEALTH AND FITNESS CHANGES WORK
Average time to form a new habit under ideal conditions: 66 days, not 21
People overestimate how much exercise they will do after a life change by an average of 3x
Dietary changes: 80% of people return to previous eating patterns within 6 months
Sustainable change requires environment redesign, not just motivation and willpower
